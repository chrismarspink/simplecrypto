Verify CSR

In public-key cryptography, the private and public keys in a pair are mathematically related in that they share the same modulus. The length of the modulus, expressed in bits, is the key length. To confirm that a particular private key matches the public key contained in a certificate signing request (CSR) and certificate, one must confirm that the moduli of both keys are identical.
